Capabilities

Everything Procure-to-Pay
demands.

Built for finance and operations teams that need real control, not another SaaS dashboard subscription.

Approval Engine

Configurable multi-step approval chains with JSONB condition rules. Route by amount, department, project, or vendor — evaluated in priority order.

3-Way Invoice Matching

Automatic line-level matching of Purchase Orders, Goods Receipts, and Invoices. Configurable tolerance controls flag exceptions before payment.

Budget Control

Real-time availability checks gate PO issuance. Track spend by department, project, or GL account with period-based budgets and rollover rules.

Vendor & Contract Management

Vendor master with compliance tracking, contract lifecycle with expiry alerts, and a tokenized vendor portal for invoice submission.

Spend Intelligence

Analytics dashboards, anomaly detection, and GL export to QuickBooks and Xero. Full spend visibility without another BI tool subscription.

Catalog & RFQ

Internal product catalog with Punchout cXML integration, request-for-quote workflows, and side-by-side quote comparison.

Immutable Audit Trail

Append-only audit log captures every mutation — who changed what, when, and why. Compliance-ready from day one, no configuration required.

Document Management & OCR

S3-compatible storage (MinIO in dev, S3/R2 in prod) with AI-powered OCR for automatic invoice data extraction and line matching.

Workflow

The full P2P cycle,
automated end to end.

01

Create Requisition

Requesters submit purchase requisitions with line items, GL codes, and project allocation. Budget availability is checked in real time.

02

Approval Engine

Configurable rules route requests through the right approvers. Sequential multi-step chains with a full audit trail. Auto-approve low-value items.

03

Purchase Order Issued

Auto-generated PO number, PDF generation, and vendor notification. Change orders create versioned PO history with complete change tracking.

04

Receive & Match

Goods receipt confirms delivery. 3-way match between PO, GRN, and invoice validates quantities and prices before AP approval.
